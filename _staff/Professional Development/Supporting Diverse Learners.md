<h3><strong>Empowering Teachers to Support Diverse Learners through Student Agency</strong></h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2026 PD/PD1.jpg">
</div>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Our teachers recently took part in a professional development session
led by Mr Choon Ming Kwan, a Master Teacher, on how to better support students
with different learning needs in the classroom. He shared practical strategies
for designing inclusive lessons, using thoughtful questioning and flexible
teaching approaches to ensure that every child remains engaged and is able
to make progress with confidence.</p>

By learning from experts and from one another, our teachers continue to
create supportive, learner-centred classrooms that nurture confident and
independent learners.</p>
