<p>We encourage parents of CCKPS to join as a Parent Support Group (PSG)
member.&nbsp;By joining the PSG, you will receive direct updates from the
school's PSG EXCO about volunteer opportunities and support needs, with
absolutely no obligation to commit to any minimum hours or specific activities.
&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;If you are keen, do fill up the details in the link (QR Code) and
our PSG EXCO member will be connecting with you.</p>
