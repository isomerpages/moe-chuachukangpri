<p>The Chua Chu Kang Primary School Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC)
enables students to reserve library resources and explore a diverse collection
of materials that enrich learning and broaden knowledge.</p>
<p>You can access the OPAC at the link below:
<br>
<br><a href="https://schoolibrary.moe.edu.sg/ChuaChuKangpri" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://schoolibrary.moe.edu.sg/ChuaChuKangpri</a>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>To reserve books, you may look at the How To Use section at the bottom
of the page. It provides instructions with pictures on how to reserve books
that can be found in the school library.</p>

<p>To access the supplementary e-resources, you may click the banner located
in the middle of the page. The page contains collated resources curated
by Civica, MOE's scholl library management partner.</p>
