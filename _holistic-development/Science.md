### **Curricular Goal**


##### **Nurture a Community of Science Inquirers**

Our school adopts an inquiry-based approach to the teaching and learning of Science. Inquiry-based learning allows our students to make observations, ask questions, think creatively and critically to investigate on a problem and make connections to science concepts.Â 

Our science curriculum provides our students with a range of learning experiences in the form of scientific investigations, authentic learning and learning journeys.
