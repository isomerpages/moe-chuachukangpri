<p>
We are excited to announce the introduction of <strong>Modern Dance CCA</strong> at CCKPS!
This Co-Curricular Activity offers students a vibrant and enriching platform to discover the joy of dance while developing their artistic talents and confidence.
</p>

<p>
Students will be introduced to a diverse range of dance genres including Contemporary Dance, Hip Hop, Street Dance, Jazz, Urban Choreography and other modern dance styles. Under the guidance of experienced instructors, they will learn dance techniques, rhythm, musicality, body coordination and expressive movement while working collaboratively to prepare performances for school events.
</p>

<p>
Participation in Modern Dance supports the holistic development of every child by improving physical fitness, flexibility, balance, coordination and strength. Students also develop creativity, discipline, resilience, teamwork, self-confidence and an appreciation for the performing arts.
</p>

<p>
Whether students are exploring dance for the first time or already have experience, Modern Dance CCA provides an inclusive environment where every child can grow, express themselves creatively and develop a lifelong appreciation for movement and the arts.
</p>

<div class="section-content">
