<p>The Malay Dance CCA provides students with opportunities to appreciate
and practice traditional Malay dances that reflect cultural heritage and
identity. Through structured training, students learn fundamental techniques,
coordination, rhythm and expressive movements while working collaboratively
as a team.
<br>The CCA nurtures confidence, discipline and resilience as students’ progress
in skills and perform for school and community events.
<br>Guided by values of Care, Integrity and Respect, students develop an appreciation
for cultural diversity while striving to bring out the best in themselves
and others, growing into empowered learners through the performing arts.</p>

<h4><strong><u>CCA Objective</u></strong></h4>
<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop students’ dance skills, physical coordination and musicality</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To instill values of discipline, respect, integrity and teamwork</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To build students’ confidence, resilience and self-expression through
performances</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To foster appreciation of Malay culture and cultural diversity</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To nurture empowered learners who strive for personal excellence</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h4><strong><u>Key Learning Experiences</u></strong></h4>
<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Tier 1
<br>Students learn basic dance postures, hand and foot techniques, rhythm
awareness and simple choreographies, focusing on discipline and teamwork.
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tier 2
<br>Students refine techniques, learn more complex routines, develop expression
and synchronization, and participate in school performances.
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tier 3
<br>Students take on leadership roles, mentor juniors, master advanced choreography
and formations, and represent the school in external performances and events.</p>
</li>
</ul>
