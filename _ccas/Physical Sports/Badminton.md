<p>Badminton CCA allows students to learn the skills and techniques required
in a badminton game.&nbsp; We participate in the National School Games
Junior and Senior Division, where students learn to build teamwork, resilience
and skills to showcase themselves throughout the tournament.</p>

<h4><strong><u>CCA Objectives</u></strong></h4>
<p></p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Develop physical fitness</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build technical and tactical skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Foster discipline and sportsmanship</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide opportunities for competition and achievement</p>
</li>
</ul>
